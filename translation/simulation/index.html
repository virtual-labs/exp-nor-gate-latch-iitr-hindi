3) Different combination of LEDs lit up for different combination of inputs.

i)When the S and R inputs are both low,feedback maintains the Q and Q' outputs in a constant state, with Q' the complement of Q.

ii)If S is pulsed high while R is held low, then the Q output is forced high, and stays high when S returns to low.

iii)If R is pulsed high while S is held low, then the Q output is forced low, and stays low when R returns to low.

iv)The R = S = 1 combination is called a restricted combination because, as both NOR gates output 0s, it breaks the logical equation Q = not Q.
